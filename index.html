My journey

How crochet entered my life

Use the dots or the buttons below to move between three mini-chapters.

1. The house of crocheters 1

Yarns were always present in my house. My mom is a skilled crocheter, and my sister is a talented knitter. So, growing up, I have always seen my mom making all the kinds of clothing, applying the styles and colors of her imagination in real life. My sister also learned how to knit in her adolescence and created many unique items.

Watching my mom and sister for years making these cute items and turning everything they imagine into reality intrigued me. My mom had always noticed that and had tried to convince me many times to crochet, but I would not listen, until that day came.

In 2020, all my studies took place online, so I stayed home almost all the time, which was boring at the beginning. One day, one of my friends came to my house and saw one of the cardigans my mom made. She was so fascinated and excited that she decided to try making something similar. When I saw that, I got really excited and decided to start learning as well.

This cardigan was handmade by my mom: my biggest inspiration.

2. The house of crocheters 2

My task was not a hard one since my mom already had all the tools in our house, which meant I did not have to buy anything. I asked my mom to teach me how to crochet, and she welcomed the idea warmly. She got me one of her yarns (which I later knew was the cheapest one) and had me learn the basics.

She told me that after getting used to the main stitches, I can start my first project. So, I spent hours on repeating the stitches I had learned again and again until I felt ready to start.

My mom suggested that I make a scarf since it has a repetitive pattern, where I would repeat the same line over and over until reaching the desired length.

In 2 days, I was done with my first project, which instead of having a rectangular shape, turned into a shape I do not even have a name for. I was proud of my first weird-looking project, and so was my mom (though I could see her muffling her laughter).

On the same day, I decided to move forward and start with a cardigan. My mom tried to convince me with something easier, but I was so adamant about it that I actually started the next day.

3. What crochet does for me now

Crocheting became a crucial part of my autumn/winter days. Making unique items and even holding the hook and yarns in my hand is what brings me peace. I once read that crocheting is an effective way of reducing stress. From that day on, whenever I feel a bit stressed, I grab my yarn and start crocheting, which in turn, slows my thoughts down.

Crocheting is more of an essential to me than a hobby. I always perform another task while crocheting, such as watching a film, or even studying. Multi-tasking brings me joy because at the end of the day, I end up with both a hat and an episode. Another reason is that studying for many hours makes me bored, so when I crochet at the same time, I kill this boredom efficiently.

Try crocheting yourself

This page is for anyone who feels curious about crocheting.

I encourage everyone reading this page to start crocheting, so you can always have your unique style embellished with your touch. I learned the basics in less than 2 hours and practiced them over the next 2 days. If I could do it, You all can do it too. With a little patience and dedication, you can become skilled crocheters like me!
